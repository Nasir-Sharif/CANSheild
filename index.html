About CANShield

Why We Made This

Modern vehicles rely on the Controller Area Network (CAN) bus for critical communication. CANShield protects against cyberattacks like spoofing, fuzzing, and DoS, ensuring safer vehicles.

Why We Use This

CANShield uses a Random Forest classifier trained on diverse CAN bus datasets for high-accuracy anomaly detection. Its web interface enables real-time intrusion detection for cybersecurity professionals.
